ECE 196 Fall 2023

Problem

Forward Head Posture is common among those who are spending many hours sitting in front of a desk, like students and office workers, which can result in having bad posture everywhere else including, but not limited to, driving and walking. Prolonged bad posture can cause kyphosis, an exaggerated forward rounding of the upper back, in severe cases.

Solution

Our solution is to create a reminder for the target audience to fix their posture whenever they veer from proper posture.
